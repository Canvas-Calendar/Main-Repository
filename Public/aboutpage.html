Our Goal

        
As engineering students, managing assignments as well as other day to day tasks can be the most important aspect of achieving 
            good grades in college. Our goal for creating Canvas Calendar is help students better understand when their assignments are due 
            not just by day, but by week or month to help students better manage their time. Features such as automatically importing due 
            dates from canvas to our assignment start date recommendation system are some of the many ways canvas calendar can help students!
