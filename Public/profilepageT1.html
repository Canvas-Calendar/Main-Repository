How To Link Your Canvas Account

Step 1: Retrieve your canvas access token

1.1: In Global Navigation, click the Account, then click the Settings.

1.2: To manually add an access token, click the Add New Access Token button.

1.3: Enter a description for your access token in the Purpose field.

1.4: You can also select an expiration date by clicking the Calendar icon.

1.5: To generate a token with no expiration, leave the Expires field empty.

1.6: To generate a new access token, click the Generate Token button.
